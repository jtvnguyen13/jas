Frequently Asked Questions

Is this for all sports?

Yes. While we use examples from different sports, the science of stress, recovery, and mindset applies to all athletes—swimmers, runners, soccer players, gymnasts, wrestlers, everyone.

What age group is this for?

Project Mindful Athlete is designed for youth athletes (ages 13-18), but the science works for any age. Coaches, parents, and even collegiate athletes find our resources valuable.

Is this scientific or motivational?

It's both—grounded in science, delivered with warmth. Every strategy is backed by neuroscience or sports psychology research. We don't use vague positivity. We use evidence.

Can I use these tools if I'm struggling mentally?

Our tools are preventative and performance-enhancing. If you're struggling with anxiety, depression, or other serious mental health challenges, please reach out to a mental health professional. These tools complement professional support but don't replace it.

How often should I use the tools?

Start with the pre-competition routine regularly. Use the reset protocol when you need it. Check in with the burnout checklist weekly. Personalize your practice based on what resonates with you.

Is there a cost?

Our core resources are free. Our mission is to make this science accessible to every athlete, regardless of background or financial situation. We may offer premium content or consulting services in the future.
